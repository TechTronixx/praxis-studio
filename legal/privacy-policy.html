Privacy policy

Last updated 23 July 2026

Praxis Studio respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

What we collect

  • Things you send us – your name, email, and message when you contact the studio.
  • Usage data – anonymous statistics about how the site is used, only if you accept optional cookies. See the Cookie policy.

Why we collect it

To reply to you, to run and improve this website, and to meet our legal obligations. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

How long we keep it

Contact emails are kept for as long as needed to work together, then deleted. Analytics data is retained for up to 13 months.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to stop using it. Email studio@praxis.studio and we will respond within 30 days.
