This policy explains how Praxis Studio uses cookies on this website, and the choices you have. It sits alongside our Privacy policy.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a site stores on your device. Some are needed for the site to work; others help us understand how the site is used. You control the optional ones through the banner on your first visit.

Your choices

  • Accept all — enables technical and optional cookies.
  • Technical only — enables only what the site needs to run.

Technical cookies do not require consent because the site cannot function without them. To change your choice, clear this site’s storage in your browser and reload — the banner will appear again.
